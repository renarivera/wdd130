Overview

Purpose

Rafting Site's main purpose is to provide detailed and up-to-date information on white water rafting activities. This includes information on the best rivers for rafting, the best times of the year to raft, the best rafting operators, and any other relevant information for those interested in this exciting outdoor activity. In addition, the website can also provide online reservations for rafting trips, making it easy to plan a trip. In short, White Water Rafting aims to be a comprehensive and reliable guide to all things related to white water rafting.

Audience

Our target customers for the rafting site are adventure seekers who are looking for an adrenaline-filled experience in the great outdoors. They are typically between the ages of 18 and 45, with a moderate to high level of disposable income. They enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and water sports. They want to experience the rush of white-water rafting and the thrill of navigating rapids with friends and family. Our audience will usually access our site through their desktop or mobile device, as they are often planning their rafting trip from home or on the go.
